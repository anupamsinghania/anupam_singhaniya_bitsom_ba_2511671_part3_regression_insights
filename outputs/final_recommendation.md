# Final Business Recommendation

## Executive Summary

The objective of this analysis was to identify the key factors associated with monthly sales across retail stores. Two simple regression models and one multiple regression model were developed and compared.

Among the models tested, the multiple regression model provides the most reliable explanation of monthly sales because it evaluates several business factors together rather than considering each factor independently.

---

## Key Findings

The analysis indicates that the following variables have the strongest association with monthly sales:

- Marketing Spend
- Footfall
- Inventory Availability Pct
- Customer Rating
- Store Type Mall


These variables were statistically significant in the final regression model and therefore provide the strongest evidence for supporting business decisions.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the leadership team should focus on the following actions:

- Continue investing in marketing activities that generate measurable customer traffic.
- Improve inventory availability to reduce missed sales opportunities.
- Monitor customer experience and satisfaction, as customer-related measures influence store performance.
- Compare store performance across different store formats and regions to identify best-performing locations and replicate successful practices.

Rather than relying on a single performance indicator, decisions should consider multiple operational factors together.

---

## Variables That Should Be Interpreted Carefully

Some variables showed weaker statistical evidence in the regression model. These variables should not be ignored, but they should not be used as the primary basis for strategic decisions without additional analysis.

Business decisions should always combine statistical findings with operational knowledge and market experience.

---

## Limitations

This analysis has several limitations:

- Regression measures association rather than cause-and-effect relationships.
- External influences such as local competition, economic conditions, promotional campaigns, and seasonal demand were not included in the model.
- The model is based only on the available dataset and may not capture every factor affecting store performance.

Future analysis could improve the model by incorporating additional operational and market variables.

---

## Conclusion

The multiple regression model is recommended for business planning because it explains monthly sales more effectively than the simple regression models. It provides management with a broader understanding of the factors associated with sales performance and offers a stronger foundation for operational and strategic decision-making.
